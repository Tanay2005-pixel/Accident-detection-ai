About CrashCall

CrashCall is a smart surveillance system powered by AI that monitors road traffic in real-time, detects vehicular accidents, and immediately sends SOS alerts to emergency services and partnered hospitals. Our system uses deep learning models to detect crashes using camera feeds, helping reduce emergency response times and potentially saving lives.
